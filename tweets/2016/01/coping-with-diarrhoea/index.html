Amidst fandom chaos with Briana’s (and Louis’?) baby, Harry switching management companies (and rumors of going solo yet again in the process), and just general fandom meltdown, RBB and SBB are back with a bathroom/cleaning setup! The bears haven’t been using stickers much lately, so it’s absolutely wonderful to see the green and blue stickers, and they’re both happy!

This time, RBB is the one on the toilet, and SBB is in charge of the toilet-bowl-brush. The book Coping with Diarrhoea (that’s the British spelling) appears to be homemade. Could they be more extra? (I’m somewhat scared to ask that…they’ll prove me wrong…)

Given that, it’s a bit interesting to have a photo that’s more likely to have been taken in LA than the UK.
