The bears kind of share the bowtie, really.

RBB is wearing a Do I Look Like I Fly Economy button. Fan reports from the filming of the Jonathan Ross Show on the 11th revealed that the boys played a game of Never Have I Ever. During this game, it was revealed that Louis, Harry, and Liam are part of the Mile High club. The fandom then went on about Larry head-canons and tiny airplane bathrooms. Are the bears trying to tell us that private planes have bigger bathrooms? Hrm…

SBB is wearing a shower cap. The only other time we’ve seen a shower cap used as a prop was the Singapore concert. At that show, RBB had shower/cleaning props, which possibly were a nod to cleaning up the Elounor relationship. It was the closest show that RBB was at for when the breakup is said to have happened.
