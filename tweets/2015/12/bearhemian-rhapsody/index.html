RBB and SBB have friends! Brown Bear has been seen before, but the Peace & Friendship Bear (PFB) is new! The bears formation and the pun Bearhemian Rhapsody reference the Queen Song Bohemian Rhapsody.

While Freddie Mercury never officially came out, it is widely thought that that is what Freddie wrote this song as. Go read the lyrics and watch the video, and focus on the implications and inner struggle they contain.
