RBB and SBB are celebrating the holidays by sending Christmas gifts. Oh man, do they hold a grudge! They are sending the 1D Potato, from the Jimmy Kimmel Live show, a potato peeler and a potato masher. YIKES! The potato originally represented JKL making fun of the fans, but it could also represent a more general nod to the fandom manipulation that has occurred for years, coming from 1DHQ.

SBB is sitting in the salon chair, which possibly has alluded to girlfriends/beards in the past. Is this a nod to Louelle? Or did SBB just need comfortable seating and didn’t want to sit on the floor?

And then we have the handwriting. It is speculated that RBB and SBB are written in different handwriting. People have also compared a few pieces of handwriting. The wedding card is Harry or Louis. (There’s a post about how Louis draws his speech bubbles like that, so possibly his handwriting too, but I can’t find that post right now. Link anyone??). The second picture is Harry’s journal.

Some folks have also taken notice that the card bears some resemblance to gifts that have appeared under the Tomlinson Christmas tree and also with Harry somewhere. Side-eye upon side-eye!
